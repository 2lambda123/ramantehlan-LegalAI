Analysis
IN THE SUPREME COURT OF INDIA
CIVIL ORIGINAL JURISDICTION
(UNDER ARTICLE 32 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA)

Dr. Akkai Padmashali & Ors (Petitioners) Vs Union of India & Ors (Respondents)

WRIT PETITION (CRIMINAL) NO. 76 OF 2016

Synopsis

  • The Petitioners (Member of transgender community) feels Section 377 of the Indian Penal Code is unconstitutional, being violative of their fundamental rights under Articles 14, 19 and 21 of the Constitution.
  • Autonomy & dignity guaranteed under Article 21; right to equality under Article 14; right to expression and freedom under Article 19.
